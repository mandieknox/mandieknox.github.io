Southern Soul. Rock and Roll.

The Devil Music Co. are a bunch of dirty troublemakers from Hattiesburg, MS.
Ranging from pop rock to indie folk, their tunes have taken the local music scene by storm.
Their latest single "Fire, Fire," from their most recent release Stay Alive,
has already been nominated for Song of the Year at the local Best of Pine Belt awards in Hattiesburg.
Give it a look, give it a listen, and hey - if you're into it - check out
the rest of Stay Alive on iTunes and Spotify!
